Family Tradition

Pat's Salmon Cookies has deep family roots in the Pacific North West. Founded in 1961, our family has been crafting delicious pastries for the people of Seattle ever since. Inspired by the majestic Sockeye Salmon, we have blended the world renouned sockeye salmon flavor and savory cookies to create our Salmon Cookies™.
