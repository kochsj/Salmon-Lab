Family Tradition

Our father and company founder, Pat Trout, was born and raised in Seattle, WA. Inspired by the majestic Sockeye Salmon, our father blended the famous sockeye salmon flavor and savory home-made cookies to create our world renowned Salmon Cookies™. Founded in 1961, Pat's Salmon Cookies has been crafting delicious pastries for the people of Seattle ever since.
